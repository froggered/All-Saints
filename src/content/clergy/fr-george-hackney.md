Born in 1941, Fr George served 8 years as a Police Officer before residential training for the Anglican ministry at the former Lincoln Theological College (1965-1968). He took his Masters in Theology at the University of Hull.

He resigned from his Anglican parish in 2003 and was received into the Orthodox Church that same year at Holy Transfiguration, Nottingham. He studied Orthodoxy for two years with the Institute for Orthodox Christian Studies in Cambridge before being ordained an Orthodox Priest in 2006 by Metropolitan Kallistos.

Fr George served for 15 years as assistant to Archimandrite Philip at All Saints of Lincolnshire. During this time he also served as Archdiocesan Ecumenical Officer, Chaplain to Morton Hall Prison, and Orthodox Chaplain to the University of Lincoln. After recovering from major heart surgery in 2019, he served as Parish Priest at St Aethelheard's, Louth for three years before retiring in August 2022.

He is married and lives in Lincoln with Khourija Annis, his wife....
