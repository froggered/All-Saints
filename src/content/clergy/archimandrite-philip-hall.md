Archimandrite Philip was ordained priest in 1995 and appointed Archimandrite in 2015. He has served as Protosyngellos (Spiritual Advisor to the Archbishop) for the Antiochian Archdiocese since 2023, overseeing the selection, initial training and monitoring of new deacons and priests.

Born in Kenya in 1959, he grew up across Africa and the Middle East before being educated in England. After 22 years as a teacher in Zambia, Birmingham, and North Lincolnshire, he was ordained in the Church of England and served as Bishop of Exeter's Chaplain and Director of Ordinands.

He left the Anglicans in 1989 and was received into the Orthodox Church in 1990. He was ordained by Metropolitan Gabriel and has served in Louth, Lincoln, Worcester, and was for four years in charge of the monastery in Gatten.
